Rules
  • Each player has 2 Rock, 2 Paper, 2 Scissors.
  • Odd rounds: Player 1 challenges. Even rounds: Player 2 challenges.
  • The challenger chooses card and arena. The responder sees only the arena.
  • Rock beats Scissors. Scissors beats Paper. Paper beats Rock.
  • Tie: both players gain one spark.
  • Loss: loser gains one spark.
  • A spark must be used next turn or discarded.
