Find chunk borders with coordinates

In Bedrock, use X and Z. Every chunk is 16 blocks wide and 16 blocks long.

In Minecraft Bedrock, there is no built-in F3+G chunk border overlay, so the reliable method is coordinates.

Rule: a chunk border starts where X or Z is divisible by 16. A corner is where both X and Z are divisible by 16. Negative coordinates work the same way: … −32, −16, 0, 16, 32 …
