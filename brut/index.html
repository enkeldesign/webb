Enkel Design explained

Enkel is a Swedish word with several meanings – among them "simple", "clear" and "uncomplicated". Enkel design is intuitive and easy to understand. It is accessible to all users and purposeful without being complicated.

Enkel design is both aesthetically pleasing and enjoyable to use. Simply a great user experience.

Extra Enkel Design

Extreme simplicity. No frills, no divs with classes, no JavaScript. Just semantic HTML and the cascade (proper CSS). Compatible with every conceivable device and accessible to every human being.

What I'm saying is that all the problems we have with websites are ones we create ourselves. Websites aren't broken by default, they are functional, high-performing, and accessible. You break them.
—Barry T. Smith

Extra Enkel Design pays homage to David Bryant Copeland's Brutalist Web Design which is raw, content-focused and user-centric at its core. It's extra enkelt and it works.

The enkel.design main page features progressive enhancement and is my attempt to strike a balance between raw web on the one hand and modern UX on the other. A balance that does not compromise accessibility – my highest design principle.
